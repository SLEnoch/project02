Experience

Photography has always been a passion of mine, ever since my first Sony Cybershot point-and-shoot camera, light pink of course. As an only child I can remember wasting away many afternoons posing my toy horses outside and laying on the grass for perspective and the perfect lighting. I would post videos on YouTube of my photos and revel in every like and comment they received.

As I grew, my passion carried into more practical uses. In high school I took three years of Digital Photography where I learned the basics of form, composition, and Photoshop editing, among other invaluable skills. I also learned how to use a DSLR camera! I now own a Canon Power Shot SX510 HS and am continuing to learn more every day.
